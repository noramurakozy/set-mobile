SHORT:

Practice and unlock achievements to become a master in SET!

LONG:
A new way of playing your favourite card game!

- Practice with the interactive tutorial and learn the rules on the way
- Personal statistics to see which of your skills needs to be improved
- Use hints or shuffle the cards if you're stuck
- Stop the game and continue later
- Personalize your gameplay by enabling or disabling elements of the game
- Unlock achivements on your way of becoming a master of SET

Bonus:
Give feedback about your gameplay experience through the in-game survey, which can be completed under 3 minutes! 
Your contribution to an improved gameplay is highly appreciated!
